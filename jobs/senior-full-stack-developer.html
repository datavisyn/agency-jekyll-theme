Senior full stack web developer

To address the increasing demand for our products, we are looking for a senior developer with a minimum of four years of work experience on client and server side. Experience with managing software products and DevOps is welcome. You will enjoy being part of a dynamic team with a flat hierarchy, a great atmosphere, and outstanding employee satisfaction.

Benefits

  • Employment in an aspiring company that is living a start-up character combined with a solid financial base
  • Contributing to cutting edge visual analysis products for drug development
  • Flat hierarchy with agile management
  • Real work-life-balance and flexible working hours
  • Work remotely for a part of your time
  • Free annual pass for public transportation
  • Cooperation with lead research facilities in the field of data visualization
  • Visit industry conferences

Required technical skills

  • JavaScript (ES 6/next) and/or TypeScript
  • Object oriented programming experience
  • HTML5 + CSS3
  • Source code management systems

Optional technical skills

  • Visualization: D3.js + plotting libraries such as Chart.js
  • Data science: data wrangling using Python, pandas, numpy, R
  • Databases: Relational, NoSQL, key-value stores, etc.
  • Deployment: Docker, CI, Kubernetes, cloud services (AWS, Azure Cloud)
  • Dependency management (webpack, npm, etc.)
  • Design: user experience, graphic design tools
  • Testing: testing strategy, unit tests for web applications, front-end tests (e.g., Selenium)

Soft skills

  • Independent and reliable team player
  • Experience in working on large-scale web-based projects
  • Excellent knowledge of German and English (all code, documents, and documentation are in English, as well as a significant part of internal and external communication)
  • Customer service oriented and prepared to interact with customers and visit customer sites
  • Strong communication skills, prepared to teach workshops and create presentations to customers
In accordance with §9 Abs. 2 GlBG: the minimum monthly salary is € 3,068.00 when employed full-time as stated in the collective bargaining agreement. The actual salary depends on your experience and qualifications and will be defined together with you. We are looking forward to hearing from you via contact@datavisyn.io.
